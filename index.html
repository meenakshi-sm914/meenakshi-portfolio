About Me

I am deeply interested in aerodynamics, propulsion systems, turbulence modeling, compressible flows, and aircraft performance optimization. My academic journey focuses on bridging theoretical fluid mechanics, advanced CFD simulations, and practical hardware implementation.

I am particularly intrigued by how propulsion systems, inlet geometries, nozzle optimization, and high-lift devices impact aircraft efficiency. My goal is to contribute to the development of sustainable, efficient, and safe aerospace systems by integrating simulation tools, control systems, and experimental validation.
